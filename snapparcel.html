Saturday is a day that will live long in the memory for Shannon based company Snap Parcel who, thanks to Bank of Ireland, were given the chance to sponsor Munster as they secured a home Champions Cup quarter final with victory over Racing 92 in front of a 26,200 sell-out at Thomond Park.
